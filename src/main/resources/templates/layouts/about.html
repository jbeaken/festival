A festival of socialist ideas

If you were excited by Jeremy Corbyn's election as Labour leader, horrified by Donald Trump, want to challenge racism and oppression, desire action over climate change or are looking for an alternative to austerity and war then Marxism Festival 2018 is the place for you.

About Us

Marxism 2018 is a four day political festival of socialist ideas hosted by the Socialist Workers Party. Thousands of activists, campaigners, trade unionists, students, writers and academics will come together at over 130 different meetings.
