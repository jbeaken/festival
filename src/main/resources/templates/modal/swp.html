Practicalities

Where will Marxism Festival take place?

Marxism 2018 takes place in the heart of London. The event will mainly be based in Student Central, Malet Street, WC1E 7HY Meetings will also take place in:

• The Institute of Education, 20 Bedford Way, WC1H 0AL

• The Friends Meeting House, 173-177 Euston Road, NW1 2BJ

These are all very close to tube and mainline train stations.

Tickets

Tickets booked in advance are cheaper.

Early bird offer: Book your ticket before Wed 28 March 2018 and get £5.00 off the ticket price.

Creche

We provide a free crèche with a range of activities available for children of different ages up to 11 years. This must be booked by Monday 18 June.

Accommodation

We can generally arrange free accommodation in London during the event. This is usually 'crash pad' type of accommodation on floors and in community centres.

Please note that although we try our best to provide free accommodation to all who need it this is NOT guaranteed and we would encourage those who can to make their own arrangements

All accommodation booked after Monday 19 June will be “crash pad” in the community centre.

Bookmarks Bookshop

Bookmarks Bookshop will be open every day during the Marxism Festival in the Library Bar in Student Central. They will have books by speakers at the event and books that complement meetings and courses.

Look for Bookmarks pop-up stalls throughout the festival and follow them on twitter for daily updates and offers: @bookmarks_books or visit them online at bookmarksbookshop.co.uk

Stalls

If you would like to book a stall for your campaign or organisation please email info@marxismfestival.org.uk

There will be a charge for stalls.

Disabled Access

We make every effort to ensure that Marxism is accessible for disabled people.

Student Central and the Friends Meeting House are fully accessible.

The Institute of Education is currently undergoing modification. They have requested that people fill in a PEEP for any meeting located in their building. This form will be sent out with all tickets and will be available at the event.
